Primary Target Personas

Our strategy focuses on two core customer segments whose needs and motivations directly align with our service offerings.

The "Aging Professional"

Age 40-65 | Urban | $80k+ Income

Pain Points: Experiences fatigue, brain fog, and low libido that impact his career and family life.

Motivations: Seeks to regain vitality, mental sharpness, and confidence. Values efficiency, discretion, and evidence-based solutions.

Age 35-50 | Fitness-Oriented

Motivations: Driven by performance optimization and longevity. Already uses supplements and tracks biometrics.

Marketing Angle: Message around data-driven optimization, enhanced recovery, and achieving peak physical potential safely and under medical supervision.
